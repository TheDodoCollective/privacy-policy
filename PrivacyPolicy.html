This Privacy Policy outlines how The Dodo collective Co Ltd collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Moris Diaries mobile application and website ("App"). By using the App, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

  • Location Data: If you allow the App to access your location, we may collect information about your location, including your GPS coordinates and IP address.
  • User-Generated Content: If you choose to submit user-generated content (e.g., reviews, photos), we may collect the information you provide, including your username, profile picture, and any other information you choose to include.
